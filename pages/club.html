FOOTBALL CLUB, PAST AND FUTURE:

Gornje Sinkovce football club was founded in 1980 as Kooperant football club. Since its foundation, the club has worn black and white football jerseys. The players who performed in the team at that time were mostly from the settlement of Gornje Sinkovce, and a couple of players were brought from the town of Leskovac.

Just one year later, the club wins the trophy in the City Cup, thus winning the first cup, which will also be the only one in history. At the beginning of the 90's, the club ceases to exist.

A new era in the club in 2015, the initiative of Miodrag Ristić and Milan Denić begins. The club begins to live again. Kooperant Football Club was renamed as Gornje Sinkovce FC with the consent of the majority. In the first season, the football club competed in the city league where it finished fourth.

The club still competes in the City League. This season, the club achieved great success by reaching the round of 16 of the District Cup, where it was eliminated by the team Jedinstvo from Gornje Stopanje, which competes in the fourth division.
